# Meowmoon Bowling v1.5.4 Train and Dragon Animations Report

## Purpose

This release adds the two approved special pin animations supplied for integration.

## Changes made

- Added `trainlinked` to `SPECIAL_ANIMATION_REGISTRY` as a 6000 ms `staticTimed` overlay animation.
- Added `dragonfirev5` to `SPECIAL_ANIMATION_REGISTRY` as a 4600 ms `staticTimed` overlay animation.
- Reused only the approved prefixed drawing functions and deterministic helpers.
- Added small game wrappers that derive elapsed time, duration, scale, and the bottom-center anchor from the hit pin.

## Animation details

- Connected Train: engine, passenger car, freight car, and caboose cross left to right with visible couplers between adjacent units.
- Friendly Dragon Fire: the dragon breathes a large non-retracting flame, leaves dense blue, purple, yellow, and warm-colored sparkles, then flies upward off screen.

## Intentionally not changed

- No gameplay, pin-count, startup-overlay, phase-state, lifecycle-helper, ball-special, or unrelated visual-design changes.
- No service-worker behavior changes beyond the required versioned app and cache references.

## Version/cache

- App file: `app-v1.5.4.js`
- Cache name: `meowmoon-bowling-v1-5-4-train-dragon-animations-cache`

## Verification

- `node --check game/app-v1.5.4.js` passed.
- Browser-tested the train crossing and automatic completion.
- Browser-tested the dragon fire sequence and automatic completion.
- Continued rolling after both animations and verified progression into a newly generated next level.
